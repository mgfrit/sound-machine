Using the Soundboard

The physical device has nine enchanted buttons — three group runes atop, and six sound runes below. Press a group rune first, then a sound rune to unleash it.

📡
Connecting Bluetooth
1
Put your Bluetooth speaker into pairing mode.
2
Hold the Bluetooth button on the device for 2 seconds. The machine will scan and pair automatically.
3
A chime confirms a successful connection. The device remembers this speaker and reconnects on every boot.
If pairing fails, ensure no other device is connected to the speaker, then try again.
🌐
Connecting to WiFi
1
If the device cannot find a known network on boot, it opens a hotspot called SoundMachine-Setup (password: soundmachine1).
2
Connect your phone or computer to that hotspot, then navigate to http://soundmachine.local:5000/wifi.
3
Scan for networks, select yours, enter the password, and tap Connect & Reboot. The device joins your network and this page becomes available on it.
